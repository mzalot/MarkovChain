Aryon took his daily trip to the chipotle. He was prepared to eat many burritos. On his way to the Chipotle, he could imagine all the burritos stuffed in his mouth. The burritos would be so juicy and tender. As Aryon arrived at the Chipotle, he did not know if he would get guac. He neared the front of the line. He loaded up his burrito with everything he could imagine. He stared at it sensually. He went to the table. He stared at the burrito. He shoved it in his mouth. Gone in five minutes. He was satisfied.
